- **0–4 s:** Variety of “before” moments — different industries, screens, tasks (no single niche).
- **4–8 s:** Flows connecting, steps completing, one abstract “automation” feel — show range, not one use case.
- **8–12 s:** L² Flows logo or wordmark, clean and minimal.
- **12–15 s:** End card: “L² Flows” + “Automate. Scale. Grow.” + lsquaredflows.agency (optional).

**Style:** Modern, minimal, tech. Blue/purple accent. Keep it generic — no single business type.
